Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish that can be whipped up quickly and easily. To make scrambled eggs, you start by cracking eggs into a bowl and whisking them together until the yolks and whites are fully blended. Next, you add a splash of milk or cream, a pinch of salt, and some freshly ground black pepper to the mixture. The eggs are then cooked over medium heat in a nonstick pan, with butter or oil, until they start to set. At this point, you use a spatula to gently stir the eggs, breaking them up into small curds as they continue to cook. When the eggs are cooked to your liking, you take the pan off the heat and transfer the eggs to a plate. Scrambled eggs can be served as is or with a variety of toppings, such as chives, cheese, salsa, or hot sauce.

One of the best things about scrambled eggs is how versatile they are. You can customize them to suit your taste preferences by adding different ingredients to the mixture or serving them with various accompaniments. For example, you can make cheesy scrambled eggs by adding grated cheddar or Parmesan cheese to the egg mixture before cooking. Or you can make a more substantial breakfast by serving the scrambled eggs with bacon, toast, and fruit. Scrambled eggs can also be made ahead of time and reheated in the microwave, making them a great option for busy mornings when you don't have time to cook from scratch. No matter how you choose to prepare them, scrambled eggs are a classic and satisfying breakfast dish that are sure to start your day off on the right foot.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Put the stove on high flame and put a wok on it.
  2. Now pour some oil and wait till it gets heated up.
  3. Now pour 3 eggs and heat them for 2 minutes.
  4. Now scramble the eggs.
  5. Dish is ready to serve.
  6. Enjoy.
