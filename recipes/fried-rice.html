Uncle Roger's Fried Rice

Description:

Chicken fried rice is a popular and delicious dish that combines the goodness of rice with the richness of chicken. This dish originated in China and has become a staple in many Asian cuisines, as well as worldwide. The dish is made by stir-frying cooked rice, along with diced chicken, vegetables, and various spices. The result is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is both filling and nutritious.

The chicken fried rice recipe typically includes vegetables such as carrots, peas, onions, and green onions. The dish is usually seasoned with soy sauce, sesame oil, and black pepper, among other spices. Chicken fried rice can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish, and it is often served with a side of vegetables or a salad. This dish is a great way to use up leftover rice and chicken, making it a practical and economical meal option. Whether you're looking for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a satisfying lunch, chicken fried rice is a classic dish that is sure to please your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Pre Cooked Rice
  • Veggies
  • Preferred Spices
  • MSG (Ajinomoto)
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Put a Wok on the stove and put the stove on high flame.
  2. Add some oil into the wok.
  3. Add 3 eggs and fry for 2 minutes on high flame.
  4. Now add 80% cooked chicken.
  5. Fry them for 2 minutes.
  6. Now add the pre cooked rice and stir completely such that egg and chicken gets perfectly mixed with rice.
  7. Now add your preferred veggies and spieces into the rice.
  8. Add the main ingredient MSG into the fried rice.
  9. Add some soy sauce for adding flavor.
  10. Your fried rice is ready to serve.
  11. Enjoy.
